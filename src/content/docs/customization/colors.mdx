**ways to customize blxshell:**
- Use Settings App most easiest separated to 6 pages
	- Appearance
	- Wallpaper
	- Bar
	- Launcher
	- Clock
	- About

- Use config at ~/.config/quickshell/config.json
	- changes will append instantly
